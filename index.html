Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

QRexact is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use QRexact.com.

We collect minimal information to operate the service:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, QR codes generated (type and count only — never the content), browser type, and general location.
  • Payment data: Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.
  • Email address: Only if you contact us or sign up for a paid plan.

2. QR Code Content

QR codes are generated entirely in your browser. The text, URLs, or data you enter are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. Your content stays on your device.

We may use lightweight analytics (such as Vercel Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data.

6. Data Retention

We do not store QR code content. Payment records are retained as required by law. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

QRexact is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at QRexact@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will post the updated version with a new date.
