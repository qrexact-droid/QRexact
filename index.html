Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

QRexact ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use QRexact.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate the service:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, QR codes generated (type and count only — never the content), browser type, and general location (country/region).
  • Payment data: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card details.
  • Email address: Only if you contact us directly or sign up for a paid plan.

2. QR Code Content

QR codes are generated entirely in your browser. The text, URLs, or data you enter to create a QR code are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. Your content stays on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the QRexact service
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To respond to support requests
  • To analyze aggregate usage trends (never individual content)

4. Cookies & Analytics

We may use lightweight analytics (such as Vercel Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting (Vercel Privacy Policy)

6. Data Retention

We do not store QR code content. Payment and subscription records are retained as required by law. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

QRexact is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at QRexact@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at QRexact@gmail.com.

By using QRexact.com, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
